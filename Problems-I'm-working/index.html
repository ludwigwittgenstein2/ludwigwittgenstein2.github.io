Problems that I'm Interested:

I follow the tradition of Natural Philosophers before 1900's. They solved all-types of problems. I'm familiar with most of the figures, problems concerned. I'm interested in real-world problems.

Natural Philosophy is concerned with study about nature. From School of Salamanca , "So knowledge was not parceled out..........the conviction that small truth is related to large truth has been eviscerated." I believe one can connect this to any discipline.

Problem-solving skills can be applied to any branch of human-knowledge. Human knowledge is all-interconnected. Leonardo Da-Vinci, Benjamin Franklin and many others followed the same pathway. I've been interested in solving some important problems. I'm going to write about them and upload my solutions here!

Water-Problem
I grew up in Tamil-Nadu, India. Water is an important resource and basic necessity.Unfortunately, It's not accessible to everyone-one. A Software Engineer or Federal Employee wakes up with no water in their apartment. Less-Rainfall, drought makes agriculture unsustainable. Many Farmers have less methods to predict rainfall or find new sources of water. Finding water is the first-step in meeting the biggest need for Agriculture. It's the heart of their living. One of the popular-methods to find water is, "dowsing" method. People make assumption about a location using a rod or stick. The farmer spends atleast $10,000 to find water. He has high-risk of losing his money. It's either boom or bust.

Goal: A low-cost effective way to find water-underground using Computational and other Engineering methods.
